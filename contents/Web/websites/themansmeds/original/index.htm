Question: Why are
Generic Drugs cheaper
than branded medicines?

Answer: Manufacurers that produce generics do not spend much money on development of a new preperation; they just need to reproduce something that was developed before. That’s why the price for generics is much more affordable. Usually generics are let to be produced only after the original medication is being sold in the market for several years.

Question: Is Generic the exact copy of the original medicine?

Answer: If we study the chemical composition of the generic and the original medicine, we’ll find out that in reality they are not identical (duplicates). They have similar “theraputic core” but additional ingredients can differ either in quantitative or qualitative composition. Anyway it doesn’t have any influence on the effectiveness of the medicine. At the end of the patient security period manufacturer’s only semi-product of their brand a formula. Manufacturer- original does not open the secret of producing.
