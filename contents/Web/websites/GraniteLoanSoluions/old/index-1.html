Our Creative Team

Chaz Guinn
Title: Portfolio Manager-Alternative Investment Division
Experience

Chaz brings in six years of experience in creating market opportunities to accelerate expansion, increase revenues, and improve profit contributions over broad industries, markets, accounts, and portfolios. Experienced in creating marketing advantage, reducing and controlling expenses, and fostering a strong culture of teamwork, shared goals, and a dedication to borrower/client/investor satisfaction. Chaz was hand selected to pioneer a department within one of the largest Private Equity firms in the U.S., to effectively manage, trade, and liquidate over 1 billion dollars of distressed residential loans. He helped build a specialized department from 2 employees to 15, to average one million a month in revenue for over three straight years. He is well versed in all aspects of REO's, Short-Sales, Commercial, Loss Mitigation, Foreclosure/Bankruptcy, Servicing, Refinances, Evictions, and lockouts. In his career, Chaz has expanded a buyer/investor data base to over 1000 national and global equity firms who participate in the U.S. distressed loan marketplace. Has traded and sold multiple different product avenues in the commercial and residential derivative markets. Expanding from CDO’s, CLO’s, RMBS, ABS, and credit default swaps.

Education

Graduated from Montana State University with a BS in Finance, with a minor in Business Administration.

Personal

He is involved in Alumnus programs through MSU and local High schools to assist the youth through education of sports and the discipline it creates for their lives. Chaz played division one collegiate football at Montana State University, where he finished in the top ten all time in Receptions and Receiving yards. After graduation, Chaz went undrafted in 2005 to the San Diego Chargers where he played for the team for one full season.

WHO ARE WE

We are premier principal seller of distressed 1 st lien whole loans.

WHAT WE DO

We acquire bulk portfolios of non-performing whole loans from the largest banks in our country and immediately re-trade the unworked loans to secondary market investors at wholesale pricing.

OUR MISSION
GLS is committed to being a solution to our countries housing crisis.

We create solution by acquiring major banks distressed assets in bulk, working with homeowners, and by selling to private regionalized note investors or non-profit organizations.

We also accommodate larger investment groups with structured sales of unworked assets so they too can become a part of the solution by offering the homeowners various workout opportunities.

We believe that together we can work towards the stabilization of our countries economic challenges by creating profits, jobs, and ultimately a stable real estate marketplace.

What Can You Expect

  • Loans that are guaranteed to be in 1 st lien position
  • High level of transparency during transactions
  • Wholesale price points
  • Constant flow of new and unworked assets to choose from
  • High level of integrity, professionalism and customer service
