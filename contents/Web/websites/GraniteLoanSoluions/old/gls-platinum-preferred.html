GLS Platinum Program

Benefits
  • 2 week marketing lead time on specific portfolios or assets for sale (AFS)
  • Unworked assets (no loss mitigation efforts taken by GLS – straight from the bank to your customers)
  • All assets will be packaged with fresh Value, Occupancy, Title, and Tax data (V.O.T.T.)
  • Closed deal - marketing compensation GLS Pricing expectations met = 1.5% of trade dollar amount
  • Sub GLS Pricing expectation = 1% of trade dollar amount
  • See attached pricing expectation sheet Buyer tracking
  • GLS will not allow your buyers to circumvent you for 6 months post platinum relationship
  • GLS will not compensate you if the buyer you bring is already vetted in our system (will provide proof when applicable)
  • Semi-exclusivity - Only 5 companies at a time will be entered into the Platinum program
  • Volume keeps you in the program (minimum of 30 assets sold per month is goal)
  • Either party can terminate relationship at any time with 30 days written notice
  • Only competition will be GLS’s Tier 1 buyers
  • Tier 1 = 3 Successful trades with minimum of 1M total trade dollar amount
  • Simultaneous Trades are available to all Tier 1 buyers that you create (inquire if interested)
Platinum Partner Responsibilities
  • Get all potential buyers vetted with GLS prior to showing them assets
  • Vetting package completed, signed, delivered to GLS and approved
  • Accommodate process
  • Pre-bid conference call with you, your buyer, and our Production Manager, Claudia Newcomer
  • Setting proper expectations to ensure smooth transactions
  • Explaining the GLS process and Q&A
  • Utilization of Dropbox for DD docs
  • Offer process
  • Delivery of original collateral files and assignments
  • Servicing transfer
  • Ensure that proof of funds are provided with final bids (1 st transaction only)
  • Make sure your buyer understands that a 10% deposit will required within 24 hours after the contract is sent to buyer (1 st transaction only)
