GLS Simultaneous Trade Platform (STP)

Programs Available
  • STP1 – Product that fits GLS’s buying criteria but can be immediately passed through to GLS T1 buyers
  • STP2 – Product that is outside GLS’s buying criteria but available to us through our bank relationships
STP 1
  • This program offers premium wholesale pricing along with the flexibility of cherry picking which ensures buyers only acquire assets that fit their specific business model
  • You are ensured assets every time because GLS has already been awarded the portfolio
  • Minimum participation level is 500K acquisition price (@GLS discretion)
STP 2
  • This program offers our lowest level of wholesale pricing and gives the buyer access to all product available to GLS through all of our banking relationships
  • All transactions are on a take-all basis (no cherry picking) excluding pre and post close kick outs
  • Minimum participation level is1.5M acquisition price (@GLS discretion)

Benefits of STP Programs

Benefits
  • Leverage GLS’s relationships to ensure consistent deal flow
  • Utilize the experience of the GLS acquisition team
  • GLS will provideV.O.T.T.(value,occupancy,title,taxes) on all assets during the process
  • GLS will handle all curative title work and/or kick outs prior to close in collaboration with buyers

  • No daisy chains, GLS only trades direct with the largest banks in the USA
  • Access to un worked loans (by anyone other than the banks themselves)
  • Buyers only pay for diligence when they successfully close the trade which eliminates lost deal costs
  • GLS utilizes an on-line secured portal for distribution of all trade due diligence documents to create a seamless and transparent transaction
