Position: Whole Loan Trader

We are looking to expand our horizons and launch ourselves into a much higher volume bracket. Granite purchases distressed residential loans in bulk from the top and most respected banks in our country. It is our goal to create a transparent purchase and sale model not only with the major institutions, but also for our consistent buying clientele. Our clients stretch both nationally, from California to Maine, and globally from Paris to Australia. Headquartered in Irvine, California we are the leading innovators in “Loan Modifications” and “Note Sale Trading”. This market serves to facilitate the secondary trading of Asset-Backed Securities (ABS), Residential Backed Securities (RMBS), Commercial Mortgage-Backed Securities (CMBS), Collateralized Debt Obligations (CDO), Collateralized Loan Obligations (CLO) and Whole Loans (both Residential and Commercial).

Make a difference by working in this dynamic, growing field for one of the most innovative and fastest-growing companies in the mortgage, secondary markets, and asset management industry. As a Whole Loan Trader, you will work alongside highly motivated, very experienced, and well-educated peers who share your passion for providing the best possible service to our homeowners, and growing client database. The position offers advancement opportunities and a competitive salary, health and dental benefits, and bonus incentives for the above-average performer.

Responsibilities will include:
  • Execute day to day note trades with note buyers
  • Utilizing and build strategic partnerships with our preferred clients, and also newly vetted clients who have not yet purchased an asset from Granite.
  • Coordinating team-oriented reports from sales, modifications, available assets, analytics, and reporting to ensure up-to-date and accurate portfolio statuses.
  • Coordinating team-oriented reports from sales, modifications, available assets, analytics, and reporting to ensure up-to-date and accurate portfolio statuses.
  • Sourcing and interacting with all client types, from Hedge funds, to pension funds, community banks, global financial institutions, non-profit organizations, etc.
  • Bringing and continuing to build your own personal database of clients to assist in the liquidation efforts of the current portfolio
Qualifications:
  • 3-5 years’ experience in RMBS Sales & Trading
  • BS/BA or equivalent experience
  • Highly experienced in multi-tasking, and the ability to manage over 50-250 accounts at any given time
  • Must be a team oriented player
  • Highly knowledgeable in the areas of structured products ; must understand the process involved in acquisitions and the analytics that goes into investing into multi-million dollar portfolio’s
