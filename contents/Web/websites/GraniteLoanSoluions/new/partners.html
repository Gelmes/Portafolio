Our Partners



Deutsche Bank - Document Custody

Deutsche Bank has been a market leader in document custody since 1992. Deutsche Bank continually invests in technology and custody resources in order to maintain their position in the market. Deutsche Bank currently manages over 3.2 million loan files and reviews 240,000 files a month.



First American - Core Logic

CoreLogic offers a broad range of products including property profiles, digital street maps, plat maps, automated valuation models, legal and vesting information, document images, profiling and lead generation tools, market analysis, statistical information, multiple listing services' applications, aerial photographs, custom research services.



FCI Lender Services - Servicing

FCI Lender Services offers a full spectrum of Private Money loan servicing, collection and foreclosure services locally or nationally. Institutional and Private Money Lenders, Private Money Brokers, and Private Money Loan Servicers of any size can handle all aspects of servicing their Hard Money Loans by partnering with the experienced staff at FCI.

  • Collections: working with borrowers to cash flow non-performing assets, and resolve delinquencies outside of the foreclosure and REO arena when possible
  • Loss Mitigation: customer based solutions (reinstatement, repayment plan or modification) and collateral based solutions (payoff, short sale, deed in lieu) collateral based solutions (payoff, short sale, deed in lieu)


Lawyers Title - Title

Founded in 1925, Lawyers Title is one of the nation's oldest and most respected title insurance companies. Today, we’re a proud member of the FNF family, which collectively represents the largest title insurance and escrow services company in the world. Backed by virtually every measure of financial strength – from assets to title claims reserves – Lawyers Title offers policyholders the safest, most secure real estate transaction possible.

In addition to title insurance, escrow and closing services, Lawyers Title offers an array of title-related services and specialty finance solutions designed to help lenders, builders, developers, attorneys and real estate professionals grow and succeed.



Thompson Reuters - Clear

CLEAR is an investigative platform designed for professionals who need information about individuals and companies. With fast access to a vast collection of public and proprietary records, CLEAR helps you find out about people and their connections.

Powered with innovative, easy-to-use tools, CLEAR offers information that is:

  • Complete – includes even comprehensive cell phone and utilities data
  • Deep – data from social networks, blogs, news and more
  • Current – live gateways to real-time data
  • Easy to use – mapping, link charts, reports, Web analytics, and more
